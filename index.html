Hi, I'm Amal

A passionate UI/UX designer with a background in visual arts and a love for creating intuitive digital experiences.

My Journey

I began my career as a freelance designer in the cryptocurrency space, creating interfaces for blockchain applications and fintech startups. This experience gave me a unique perspective on balancing complex functionality with user-friendly design.

After several years in the industry, I decided to formalize my education by completing the Google UX Design Certificate, which helped me refine my process and expand my skill set.

Today, I combine my technical knowledge with creative problem-solving to design digital products that are both beautiful and functional.

My Design Philosophy

I believe that great design is invisible. It should guide users intuitively, solve real problems, and create delightful experiences without calling attention to itself. Every pixel has a purpose, and every interaction should feel natural and rewarding.
